Carcassonne Castles Instructions

The goal of the game is to make Castles! Two players will take turns trying to build their castle empire!

Each turn consists of 2 phases.

Phase 1

Drag and drop your tile. Use the Rotate Button to best position your tile Press the Place Button

Phase 2

If a castle is empty, claim it as your own and click on it to place a meeple inside!

Once a tile is claimed it cannot be occupied by another player.

Press the Done button to end your turn

Scoring

Each time a castle is complete, points will be given! The person with the most Meeples on a castle will claim all its points. A castle is worth the double the amount of sides it has paired
