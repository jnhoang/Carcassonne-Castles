Carcassonne Castles

Instructions

The goal of the game is to make Castles! Two players will take turns trying to build their castle empire! Each turn consists of 2 phases.

Phase 1

Drag and drop your tile. Use the Rotate Button to best position your tile. Then press the Place Button to place your tile down.

Phase 2

Castles can be claimed by adding Meeples inside! Press the Done button to end your turn.

Once a castle is complete, the meeples inside will be returned to your meeple pool.

Scoring

Each time a castle is complete, points will be given! The person with the most Meeples in a castle will claim 2 points for every tile used in the construction of the castle.

The second player will receive just 1 point per tile.
